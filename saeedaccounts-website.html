FAQ

Questions I get asked
before every engagement

Am I locked into a long-term contract?
Absolutely not. All my engagements are month-to-month. You can cancel at any time with 30 days' notice. No termination fees, no penalties — I believe in earning your business every single month.
How do I securely share my bank statements?
We use your accounting software's bank feed (QuickBooks or Xero connects directly to your bank securely). For statements, I use encrypted file-sharing portals. Your bank login credentials are never needed or requested.
What if my books are years behind?
This is the most common situation I encounter — and exactly what my Cleanup & Catch-Up service is for. I've worked on backlogs of up to 3 years. No judgment. I assess the scope, quote you a fixed price, and get it done fast. Most cleanups are completed within 5–10 business days.
Do you work with businesses in different time zones?
Yes — I work with clients in the US (EST/PST), UK, Australia (AEST), and the UAE. I'm flexible with communication hours and respond to messages within 24 hours regardless of time zone. Most work is done asynchronously so time zones are rarely an issue.
What software do you work with?
I'm certified and experienced in QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, Xero, SAP Business One (FICO & Logistics), Zoho Books, Odoo, and Peachtree. I also use Advanced Excel for dashboards and reports. If you use different software, ask me — I'm likely familiar with it.
Will my accountant/CPA be able to use your work?
Absolutely. I prepare books specifically to be tax-ready — meaning your CPA or accountant receives clean, categorized, reconciled books that reduce their time and your cost at tax season. I can also communicate directly with your accountant if needed.
How do I pay for your services?
I accept payment via Upwork (safest, with built-in protection), direct bank transfer, PayPal, or Wise for international clients. Monthly retainer clients are invoiced on the 1st of each month, payable within 5 days.
What do I need to get started?
Just book a free 15-minute call. I'll ask about your business, your current software, and what's giving you the most headaches. After the call, I'll send you a proposal within 24 hours. If you're happy, we sign an NDA and I start within 48 hours. That's it.
Do you handle UAE VAT and FTA compliance?
Yes. I have direct experience with UAE VAT law, FTA portal filing (VAT201 returns), input/output tax reconciliation, reverse charge mechanisms for imports, and audit-ready record keeping. I'm currently managing VAT for a UAE-based trading company.
Can I switch from my current bookkeeper?
Yes, and it's easier than you think. I handle the transition, review your existing books for accuracy, and pick up from where things are. Most transitions are completed within a week with zero disruption to your business operations.
